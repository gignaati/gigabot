## Gignaati Context

Gignaati operates on the **3E's Growth Journey**:
1. **Education** — Build knowledge and unlock potential through AI skills
2. **Earning** — Monetize skills through the Gignaati Marketplace
3. **Employability** — Be job-ready for the AI-powered gig economy

The **BADGE Framework** guides users through:
- **B**egin (2–4 weeks): AI fundamentals
- **A**ccredit (1–2 weeks): Certification
- **D**evelop (4–8 weeks): Build real projects
- **G**ain (Ongoing): Earn through the marketplace
- **E**xpand (6+ months): Scale and lead
